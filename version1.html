Etch is powered by blockchain technology and smart contracts. Here are some of the features:

  • All bonus conditions are stored on the blockchain.
  • No off-chain databases are used.
  • The conditions for bonuses are all stored in a smart contract which is visible to anyone.
  • All the conditions are visible via the blockchain. Hence anyone can audit the transaction conditions using for example Remix.
  • Etch is fully committed to emerging technologies, such as Polkadot to allow scaling and better security.
